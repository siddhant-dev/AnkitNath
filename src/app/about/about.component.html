03. Thermal Cycling test of Raspberry Pi camera for space environment


I was involved in this project while I was working at Aalto University on FORESAIL 1 mission. The thermal testing of the camera was carried out for verifying its reliability in a space environment. Extreme temperature ranges creates stress that affects the behavior and lifetime of the components. The problems that can be caused by temperature swings are :

  • Delayed image capture of the RPI Cam
  • Changes in electrical behavior

The Raspberry Pi camera was tested in two temperature ranges using the thermal cycling test chamber. The first test was made in the temperature range : -20℃ to +60℃ , while the second test was made in the temperature range : -40℃ to +80℃. The Raspberry Pi camera was supplied 5V using the dual power supply by Agilent 34970A and the respective current consumption of the RPI Cam was measured during operation.
